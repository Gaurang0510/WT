🖥️ Microprocessors

A microprocessor is a compact, multipurpose integrated circuit (IC) that acts as the central processing unit (CPU) of a computer, containing arithmetic, logic, and control functions on a single chip to fetch, decode, and execute instructions from binary data, forming the "brain" for devices like PCs, smartphones, and servers.
